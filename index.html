About the Department

Leading innovation at the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science to create transformative solutions for society's greatest challenges.

Established in 2018, our department has rapidly become a center of excellence for AI and Data Science education and research. We combine rigorous theoretical foundations with practical, hands-on experience to prepare students for leadership roles in industry and academia.

Our curriculum is continuously updated in collaboration with industry partners to ensure our graduates possess the most relevant and in-demand skills. We emphasize ethical AI development, interdisciplinary collaboration, and real-world problem solving.

Research & Innovation

Pushing the boundaries of knowledge through interdisciplinary research that addresses real-world challenges and creates meaningful impact.

Our research spans multiple domains including healthcare, climate science, finance, and social good. We collaborate with leading institutions worldwide and maintain strong industry partnerships to ensure our research remains relevant and impactful.

Students actively participate in research projects from their first year, gaining hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies and methodologies under the mentorship of our faculty.
